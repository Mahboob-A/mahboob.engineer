# Contact Policy Corpus Notes

Use this file to improve dynamic answers for contact, hiring, consulting, and
partnership questions.

## Good Reasons To Contact

- Backend or platform roles.
- Django, DRF, FastAPI, Redis, PostgreSQL, AWS, Docker, or system design work.
- Taply partnership or white-label conversations.
- Focused consulting around backend architecture or performance.
- Open-source or writing conversations with a clear topic.

## Not A Fit

- Frontend-only or mobile-only roles.
- Crypto or NFT outreach.
- Generic agency sales.
- Bulk recruiter blasts without role context.

## Preferred Context In A First Message

- Role or opportunity summary.
- Company stage and team size.
- Remote, hybrid, or on-site expectation.
- Stack and system ownership scope.
- Timeline and response expectations.
